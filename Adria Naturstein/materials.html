Materials

Limestone

Pure limestone has a light whitish hue. Limestone consists of tiny opaque crystals invisible to the naked eye. When adding iron minerals, limestone turns yellowish or reddish. Limestone is very suitable for all types of processing. Therefore, according to the investor's whishes, we are producing various exterior and interior items, such as stone slabs for floors and terraces, carved stone for walls, stone for areas around swimming pools, steps, columns, sinks, fireplaces, bathrooms, tables, benches, various signs written in stone.

Granite

Granite is a natural stone, and in our offer you can find different types of it from all around the world. Granite tiles (marmette), standard thickness 1cm, are used for lining walls and floors, in interior and exterior design. The type most commonly used is burnt granite tiles with anti-skid finish, but the polished ones are used rather often, too. Standard tile dimensions are 61 x 30,5 x 1 cm and 30,5 x 30,5 x 1 cm.

Kavala Mosaic – New offer!

Kavala mosaic is an ideal asset to every garden. It is most commonly used for tiling gardens, access roads and terraces.

Granite blocks

Granite blocks are used for paving exterior surfaces such as gardens or streets and they are an interesting detail in every garden patch. We offer two types: 10 x 10 x 10 cm or 10 x 10 x 5 cm.

Granite tiles

Granite tiles of different kinds, dimensions, thickness and final finis (surface processing can be polished or burnt – anti-skid finish) Granite tiles or marmette tiles are used for interior and exterior paving and they come in two varieties: polished or burnt. Their installation is as simple as ceramic tiles installation. However, due to the characteristics of granite, they last much longer and are simple for maintenance because of their cleanliness and shine. The most commonly used dimensions are 30,5 x 30,5 and 60(or 61) x 30,5 and 40 x 40 cm with some Spanish products.
